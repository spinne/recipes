Grilled Cheese Sandwich
	Butter
	Cheese
	Toast
	-
	Pan with lid

	1. Put the pan on the oven and set it to low heat
	2. Butter one side of each toast, put the first toast with the buttery side down in the pan.
	3. Add a thin slice of cheese onto the toast in the pan.
	4. Put the second toast slice onto the cheese with the buttery side looking up.
	5. Put the lid on the pan and wait until the butter on top of the toast is melted. The bottom toast should be gold-brown.
	6. Flip the entire sandwich and wait for a little while until second toast is golden-brown as well.
	7. Done.
